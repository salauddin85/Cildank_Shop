Frequently Asked Questions

Your go-to source for all your clothing inquiries at Cildank

Orders & Shipping

You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Orders' section. You'll find real-time updates on the status of your shipment.

We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. Express shipping costs vary depending on your location and will be calculated at checkout.

Delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping option selected. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 2-3 business days.
Returns & Exchanges

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase, provided it is unworn, unwashed, and in its original packaging. Please visit our Returns Center to initiate a return.

Yes, we offer exchanges for different sizes. If the size you want is out of stock, we will notify you via email, and you can choose to receive a refund instead.

To return an item, visit our Returns Center on the website, enter your order number, and follow the instructions. You can print a prepaid return label to send the item back to us.
Payments & Discounts

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. You can also pay using store credit if you have a Cildank account.

Yes, we frequently offer discounts and promotions. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay updated on the latest deals.

Only one discount code can be applied per order. However, you can combine a discount code with store credit or gift cards.
Product Information

Each product page includes a size guide. You can also refer to our comprehensive size chart available in the footer of the website for more detailed measurements.

We strive to display product colors as accurately as possible. However, due to differences in monitor settings, the color of the actual product may vary slightly from what you see on your screen.

Our clothing is made from a variety of materials, including cotton, polyester, and sustainable fabrics like organic cotton and recycled polyester. Specific material information can be found on each product page.
Account & Privacy

To create an account, click on the 'Sign Up' button at the top of the page, fill out the registration form with your details, and submit it. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

If you've forgotten your password, click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

We take your privacy seriously and use advanced encryption methods to protect your personal information. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
Sizing & Fit

Use a flexible measuring tape to measure your bust, waist, and hips. Compare your measurements with our size chart to find the best fit. Instructions and diagrams can be found on our Size Guide page.

If you are between sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for a more comfortable fit. You can also check the product description for any specific fit notes.
